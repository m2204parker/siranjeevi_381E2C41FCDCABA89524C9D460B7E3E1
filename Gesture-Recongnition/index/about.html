About Silent Talk AI

Silent Talk AI is an advanced sign language recognition system designed to bridge communication gaps. Our AI-driven gesture translator converts hand movements into text and speech, enabling seamless interactions.

Whether you are deaf, hard of hearing, or simply interested in learning sign language, our platform provides a powerful and intuitive experience.

"Empowering Conversations, One Gesture at a Time."
